Rapkat's Special Fried Rice

Description

Fried Rice with chicken.

Wikipedia Link Image

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of white rice
  • 1/2 lb of chicken
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Fry the egg
  2. Add the rice
  3. Add cooked chicken
  4. Add the sauces
  5. Fry until cooked
